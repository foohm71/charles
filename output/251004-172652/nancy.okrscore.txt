### Scoring Methodology and Rationale
The overall objective score is calculated by taking the weighted average of the completion percentages of each Key Result. Each KR is assigned a weight based on its importance to the overall objective. The scores are then multiplied by their respective weights and summed to get the final objective score. This method ensures that more critical KRs have a greater impact on the overall score.
